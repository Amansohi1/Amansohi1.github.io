Blog: Animals of India

Explore the incredible wildlife of India, home to some of the most diverse and fascinating animals on Earth!

Bengal Tiger

The Bengal Tiger is India's national animal and an iconic symbol of strength and grace. Found primarily in national parks like Sundarbans and Ranthambore, these majestic creatures are a key part of India's rich biodiversity.

Read more about Bengal Tigers

Asian Elephant

Known for their intelligence and strong familial bonds, Asian elephants can be seen in the forests of Kerala, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. They play a vital role in India's cultural and ecological heritage.

Read more about Asian Elephants

Indian Peacock

The Indian Peacock, India's national bird, is famous for its dazzling plumage and vibrant courtship displays. These birds can often be found in rural areas and near forests.

Read more about Indian Peacocks

Snow Leopard

Found in the icy Himalayas, the elusive Snow Leopard is a symbol of the fragile mountain ecosystem. These rare and beautiful predators are known as 'Ghosts of the Mountains'.

Read more about Snow Leopards

Indian Cobra

A revered and sometimes feared creature in Indian culture, the Indian Cobra is often associated with myths and folklore. These snakes play a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance.
